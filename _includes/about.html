Robot-Assisted Living Laboratory is a state-of-the-art robotic laboratory lead byDr. Jihong Zhu.We are based in theInstitute for Safe AutonomyUniversity of York, the UK. Our lab is committed to advancing the capabilities of robots to empower individuals with diverse abilities in their daily lives. Through a multidisciplinary approach, we combine expertise in control and machine learning to develop robots that seamlessly integrate with the environment and intuitively respond to user needs.
